Abstract

        We use electric appliances and electronic device in our diary and these are performed the computational processing or data processing such as digital signal processing in their inside.
        In this BIOMOD2015, we focused the digital information processing of computer. The computer recognizes all information on a combination of “1”-“0” processes. In our projects, we made A/D converter that converts analog signal to digital signal for realizing digital information processing with DNA by using DNA logic circuits. In other words, we design the translator that converts DNA information to computer language.
        The realization of our translator may become bridge that connects two systems between bio-chemical system and electronic system. Therefore, we can convert all internal signals of human to digital signal by sharing digital language between both systems.
